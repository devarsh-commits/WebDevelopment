Depression is a serious mental illness that negatively affects how you feel, think, and act. Seeking help is important.

Symptoms

Repetitive thoughts or fears
Engaging in repetitive behaviors
Distress when routines are disrupted

OCD is a disorder where people have intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and feel the urge to perform repetitive behaviors (compulsions) to reduce anxiety.

Symptoms

Difficulty focusing or staying on task
Forgetfulness and trouble organizing tasks
Easily distracted by surroundings
Trouble completing tasks or following instructions

ADHD is a neurodevelopmental disorder affecting focus, impulsivity, and hyperactivity. It can impact daily life, work, or studies.

Symptoms

Flashbacks or nightmares about the traumatic event
Avoiding situations that trigger memories of the event
Emotional numbness or detachment from others
Hypervigilance (always feeling on edge)

PTSD develops after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event, leading to emotional distress and flashbacks.
